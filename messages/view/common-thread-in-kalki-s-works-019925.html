In almost all his stories, the hero will be hunted
for by someone or
some group and the hero will be hiding or running to
save himself.

PS - VT runs most of the time, either from
Pazhuvetarayar or the
abathuthavigal.
Magudapathi - He is a freedom fighter and hence is
in hideout
throughout the story. Also, the vilains chase him to
kill him
Solaimalai ilavarasi - Same as above. Freedom
Fighter and hence is in
hideout.
Kalvanin Kadhali - kekave venam. Being a thief the
hero is in hideout.
Alai Osai - Again Freedom struggle, so hideout.

Another common thing is - all stories will have a
water body being
descibed. Either a stream or river or kulam or odai.

There will be surely a scene related to food,
eating.

Even in Kalvanin Kadhali, Kalyani's athai is
(partially) deaf.
