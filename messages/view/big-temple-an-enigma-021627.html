Re THANJAI BIG TEMPLE / LINGAM INSTALLED, we have already discussed
several factors which include ::

1. The Peruvudayar was installed first (in about 21st Year - 1006
AD) and worshipped before consecreation took place on the 285th day
of 25th Yr of RRC (1010 AD).

insctiptions do mention poojas being performed in this temple prior
to 1006 AD. Here there are two versions.

i) Pooja to Peruvudayar since 1006.

ii) PERUVUDAYAR NOT INITIATED (Not Energised, but positioned in
1006). In the Southern side miniature type temple, poojas were
performed !

2. Those who had opportunity to see the Mezanine and upper floors
(Saandaram) above Peruvudayar can understand it goes like ( Cone
icecream cone - invereted) with hollow space inside.

ASI Sriram - our beloved Member - postulated that the Lingam would
have been positioned, sands filled above - like cone ice developing
layers - and brick structure raised (using elephants moving on the
cuved-slopes & NOT STRAIGHT RAMPs - as National Georgraphy shows in
its Coverage !), SIKARA (80 ton stone structure made of 8 pieces of
about 10 ton each)was positioned above which about 13' long KALASAM
positioned !


3. Whether the Avudayar is single piece or multiple piece. This
subject - again drew controversies - appeared in varalaaru.com.

Dr. Kudavoil told the Avudayar is single piece.
Dr. Kalaikkovan informed that it is made of multiple pieces.

But both are correct !

Let us wait and see how.
