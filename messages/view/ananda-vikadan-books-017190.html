ananda vikadan magazine has one of the most succesful publishing
houses today.
their brittanica encyclopedia is world class.

but the origin of the publishing house for books( other than
magazines) originated with vasan himself.
the main loser was kalki.
kalki when under the employ of anandavikadan ( at salaries ranging
from 100- 400 with a ford car) wrote a lot of articles , stories
and
reviews for ananda vikadan.
in fact there were accusations prevalent at that time that he
forced
out other's articles as well as made them print without a by line.
pudumai pithan has much to say about it. in fact the best works of
devan were immidetly after kalki left AV.

after kalki left AV he wanted to group his writings and publish
them
as books. however legal opinion deterred him. as he was under an
employment those days the inntelectual property of his writings
were
shared by both the magazine and him.

kalki wanting to avoid a direct confrontation with the media
moghul
vasan tried a small trick. he made sinna annamalai of tamil pannai
to
print a book with some of his ananda vikadan articles.
a raging vasan threatened to sue. a profound apology was given and
all was forgiven.
now kalki wrote straight to vasan with the request. kalki had in
the
sarting years of his personal magazine criticised almost every act
of
vasan.
hence vasan wrote back saying he had starting a publishing house
in
mind and could not spare kalki's articles for a similar purpose.
several years after kalki died vasan phoned up his son rajendran
and
told him the rights now belonged to rajendran as the legal heir. a
clever rajendran asked for it in writing and got it.
however ananda vikadans publishing house was not started till much
later. i think it happened after vasns death.

now it is a mega hit.
