To add a few more things

In Nirvana Nagaram he introduced so many things the readers through Sivaraj
Charecter. To name the few which I liked the most

1. Ungalil Yethunai perukku Sislian Open teriyum?
2. Panduvarali Rahatirkkum Kamavardhini Rahatirkkum Vidhyasam teriyum?

One can see Sujatha in both his Ganesh (Sujatha's brilliance) as well as
Vasanth (Other Side of Sujatha) Characters

When we talk of paperless office (Automated Office) in 90s. He introduced
that to his readers in Pirivom Sandhipom (Rathna talks about her paperless
office in US)

Kagidha Changiligal will make me cry even today!

Manikkavum Idhu Kadayin Arambam Alla -- All these show that he was FAR AHEAD
of his times.

Sujatha Rengarajan -- We will miss you sir!

~ Udanx
