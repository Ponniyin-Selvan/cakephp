Rotary Club adopting the Natham Parameswari Mangalam Village for upliftment.

by Chandrasekaran (plasticschandra), on Thu, Jan 3 2008, 14:50 IST

Dear REACH FOUNDATION members and patrons,PS members,
This is an invitation for the the inauguration of Rotary Community
Corps, at Natham Parameswari Mangalam, of ECR Road near Kalpakkam. The
ECR road comes across a junction which shows a road on your right
taking you to Madhuranthakam. Just get into that road and ask for
Natham Village, even a kid can guide you.
Dr. T. Satyamurthy, founder trustee of REACH FOUNDATION is chief guest.
P.P. Rotarian - Chandramohan will be the Guest of Honour.
KS Ramachandran- Chairman, Lathur Union, inaugurates
Srimathi Sumathi Sivagnanam - Counsillor and N.Arul, Panchayat
President, I. Arumugam, Panchayat Vice President will grace the occasion.
Timing: 4pm Saturday 5th January,

This invitation is given by Rotarian K.Karthik,
President of Rotary Club of Madras Central Aditya.

Our dear trustee and member P.N.Subramaniam had taken all the efforts
for this to happen.

Please grace the occasion.
The temple you would see is breadth taking and statues really
awe-spiring!!

Those who want to know on the Parameswari Mangalam Natham temple visit
our bloghttp://templesrevival.blogspot.com
