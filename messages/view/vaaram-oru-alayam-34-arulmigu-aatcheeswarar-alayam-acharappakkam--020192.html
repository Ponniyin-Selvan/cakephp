This week's Podcast is about "Arulmigu Aatcheeswarar(parkapureeswarar) Alayam,Acharappakkam.

This
sivasthalam is located between chengalpattu and Dindivanam,Next to
Melmaruvathur. This is the 29th of the 32 Tevara Stalams in the Tondai
region of South India. This temple in Tondai Naadu, received endowments
from the Imperial Cholas, as seen from the inscriptions from the period
of Raja Raja Chola II.

Main Deity : Aatcheeswarar,Parkapureeswarar,mullaikanathar
Goddess : Ilankiliyammai,SoundaryaNayaki
Holy tree : sarankonrai
Pathigam : Thirugnana Samandhar

Darshan time : 6.30 am – 11.30am ,4pm – 8.30pm
Theertham : Sangu Theertham,SimhaTheertham
Festivals : Aruthra,Sivarathri,navarathri,Peruvizha during chittirai,Vinayagar Saturthi



Saint
Agathiyar,on the way to this sthalam rested in a Village called "Perum
Peru Kandigai" where Lord Aatcheeswarar gave Dharshan to him on his
request.This is also celebrated on 11th day of chittirai Peruvizha.
