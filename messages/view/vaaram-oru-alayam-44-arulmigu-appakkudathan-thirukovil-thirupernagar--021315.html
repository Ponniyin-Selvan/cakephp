This weeks episode is about " Arulmigu Appakkudathan Thirukovil,Thiruppernagar".

AudioLink :


http://podbazaar.castmetrix.net/...ruppernagar.mp3





Location&Direction:This
Divya Kshetram is located closer to Thiruchi.it's 8 kms from pudhalur
railway station and 4 miles from kallanai.

Current Name : Koviladi
MainDeity : Appakkudathan
Godesses : indradevi
Theertham : indra theertham
Vimanam : indra vimanam


Legend
says that this temple is older than srirangam temple ,hence the name
Koviladi (adi given to srirangam).it's one of the 5 arangam
temples(appala rangam).Nammazhawar got motcham after he sang about this
perumal in this temple.There is also a legend associated with this
temple where Lord narayana came as a old brahmin in disgiuse to eat
Appam from king Upamanyu.
