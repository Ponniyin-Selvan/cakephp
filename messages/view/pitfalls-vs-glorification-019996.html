drarunkrishnan@ wrote:

I think Nationalism has to be seen in the context of the time that
the story is set in. Nationalism in our current time means the
geopgraphical entity of India. In the Chola/Chera/Pandya/Pallava
times, nationalism meant a love for the respective kingdoms. In
that sense VanthiyathevAn etc were Chola nationalists while even
Ravidasan and co were Pandya nationalists.

Cheers, Arun
