Very very interesting to read, In fact I have twice visited this Virattaneswaram in Thirukoviloor which is also a Great Vaishanavaite Centre. This Temple is the Place where the Lord Ganesh appeared in  frontof Tamil Sangam Poet Avvai Piratti, and also the Place where  Angavaiand Sangavai's Marriage took Place.
 
This is also famous for the Meyporul Nayanar who allowed his enemy to kill him as he had come in the costume of Sivanadiyar.
 
This Temple is highly Vibrant for those who born under male stars.
 
Temple  is still intact and has very sharp  sculptural Characteristics.
