Sons of Rajaraja

by S.Gokul (seshadrigokul), on Sun, Jun 6 2004, 21:56 IST

The exact count of Rajaraja's children - for that matter many later
chola king's children - is unclear.

I've always thought that RR could have had more than one son.
Kudanthai N. Sethuraman cleared my doubt. He introduces atleast one
more son of Rajaraja. We are - rather unfortunately - unable to get
the name of this son. Only his title is available : Gangai kondan -
suggesting that he participated in ganges expetition.

Whose son is he ? We do not know - but Kundanthai suggests he might
be panchavan madevi's son. With due respects, it is rather a far
fetched assumption in my opinion.

This guy lived along with rajendra / rajadhiraja and was held in
high respect and was ruling certain regions of chola territory. His
son - again only the title is available - by name Alavandhan (!) -
was given the pandiyan territory to rule and so on..

We know very little or nothing about one of rajaraja's daughter
(madhevedigal) and even rajendra's daughter (arumozhi nangai)..

This is again a sample. There are many things like this in tamil
history waiting to be uncovered...

Anbudan
G
