This week's Podcast is about "Arulmigu Thirumaakaraleeswarar Alayam – Thirumaakaral".


This Shivasthalam can be reached by bus enroute via Kanceepuram - Uttarameroor .
We can see the temple from the main road just after Orikai village.




Main Deity : Thirumaakaraleeswarar, Udumbeeswarar, Agatheeswarar


Goddess : Thribuvana naayaki

Holy tree : Lime

Pathigam : Thirugnanasambandar,

Theertham : Agni Theertham



Temple Timings: Morning 8:00 Am – 12
noon and from evening 5:30 Pm – 8:00 Pm

Am, Noon pooja 12:00 Pm, Evening Pooja 6:00 Pm, Night Pooja 8:00
