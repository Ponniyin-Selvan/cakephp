Essentially ::

1. Tiruvalanchuzhi Swetha Vinayagar - Vellai Pillayar - is mentioned
from Kulothunga period only - not in RRC and RJC;

2. Ekaveeri Kaali figures in RRC and RJC period.

3. Ganapathiyar (Valanchuzhi Vinayagar in South West) is of RJC
priod;

4. Kshethra Baalar was inside the main complex, but was brought out
and a separate temple was constructed by OLAGAMADEVIAR @
Danthisakthi Vidangi during RRC period;

5. This is First of such major temple for a Parivaara Devatha -
Kshethra Baalar. "Kshethra Baalar means main diety of the locale."

6. Main difference between Kshetra baalar and Bairavar:: Bairavar is
mostly with Vahanm (Dog). Kshetrabaalar - does not have. Accessories
vary.

7. A very rare phenomenon that ONLY THE MONARCHs' Family made all
the grants. 3 grants by those who were very closely associated with
the Monarchs - were permitted, but not in big scale.

8. Grants of Sundara's wives (other than Vanavanmadevi) -
Sembianmadevi - Uthama - 3 sons-in-law of SM Devi, RRC, RJC, and
those followed the hierarchy have poured money into this temple
surprisingly.

9. RRC and RJC co-ruled for 3 yrs and the theory that "Thila
parvatham pukaruli" by RJC in memory of RRC's first Anniversary is
disputed. Possible that RJC performed this for his attaining
Sivalogam!

A must read Book. Priced at Rs. 120/- Varalaru 15th Issue is also
put on Sale.
