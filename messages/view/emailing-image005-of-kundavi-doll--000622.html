Hi All,

Yesterday we had a meet at Kalki office. Most of the PS Muthal
Yaththirai ParivaarangaL had come with snaps. All have come out well.
Mr. Vishwaksenan has taken some of the snaps for the next issue. In
that, the Devaragasiyam is going to be publicised and it will become
oorarinja ragasiyam then

Chtkala Madam had come to the meet. We had been to her home to see
her wonderful work. She showed us the miniatures of all PS
characters..Wow!! What an excellent work she has done!!!! Apart from
just the doll, the delicate details she has taken care in her work..
that was really amazing!!! The postures, the expressions, the
costumes and the ornaments!!!
How artistically she has designed each and every character!!!
thindumEl sAintha oyyara kundhavai, kazhuththu sangu maalayudan
poonguzhali, veera vaaLudan nandhini, Kayil viLakkudan Manimehalai,
sundarachola ( he is so handsome as his name and as described in PS)
and especially his crown, Vanama Dhevi, munvazhukkai matrum siRu
thoppaiyudan Azhvaarkadiyaan, kambiiramana PazhuvEttaraiyarkaL,
viyAbAri vEdaththu aruLmozhi, kuRuvaaLudan namma hero vandhiyadevan,
Karikaalan, Ravidasan etc.

Sivagamiyin Naattiya MuthiraikaLum miga azhagu..

Her interest, her sincerity, her dedication and her patience in doing
such a work are highly appreciable. The outcome of all her
effort, "THE DOLLS", are lovely and lively.. We were speechless..

Thanks to Chitkala Madam and Sir for their time and patience in
showing us the great work..

Krupa intha meeting-kku varaathathaagaiyaal he is being demoted from
VandhiyaDevan to Pinagapani/kandhamaran/parthibendhiran whomever he
wishes (atleast he has been given a chice?!) - varukaiyaaLarkalin
thIrmaanam.

- Sathya
